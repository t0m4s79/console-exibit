Neo Geo Pocket & Neo Geo Pocket Color

Description

One of the most notable arcade games companies responsible for many famous series of games SNK decided to try its feet in the portable console market, giving birth to two consoles the Neo Geo Pocket and its successor the Neo Geo Pocket Color. Both these have the same physical structure having an A, B, and options button on the right side,8 direction D-pad, a power button, and the speaker on the left, in the bottom of the device you can find a volume wheel headphone jack the battery charger port, and on top a link cable port to connect to another neo geo pocket. Although the SKN try on the portable console market was a good one it fell short of what was needed to survive long term.

History

Neo geo pocket also known as NGP is a japan and Hong Kong exclusive portable console released by SNK, a Japanese company known for many series of games like Metal Slug, Fatal Fury, in 1998 and had a short lifespan being discontinued next year for its poor sales. During its short life, 10 games were released, all of them compatible with the next edition of the NGP the Neo Geo Pocket Color. The Neo Geo Pocket Color is the successor to the NGP released in Japan, the United States, and Europe in 1999 and it was discontinued in the year 2000 for the USA and Europe, and in 2001 for Japan. In the 2 years that the Neo Geo Pocket was being sold, there were a total of 83 games released in total. The failure of both of these consoles can be traced to many factors one of the major ones was the total dominance of the portable console market by two behemoths, Sega and Nintendo.

    • CPUs: Toshiba TLCS900H core (16-bit), 6.144 MHz, Z80 at 3.072 MHz for sound.
    • RAM: 12 k for 900H, 4k for Z80
    • ROM: 64 kbit boot ROM
    • Interfaces: SIO 1 channel 19200 bit/s, 5-pin serial port
    • Resolution: 160x152
    • Colors: 16 palettes per plane, 48 palettes. 146 colors on screen out of 4096.
    • Sprites: 64 sprites per frame, 4 colors per sprite.
    • Scrolling: 2 scrolling planes, 8x8 character tiles, 4 colors each.
    • Sound: PSG 6 tone simultaneous output. Stereo sound.
    • Cartridges: Maximum 4 MB (32 Mbit) with 4-16 Mbit flash memory.
    • Batteries: 40 hours on 2 AA batteries. Lithium CR2032 battery backs up memory and clock.
